What it is

  1. 01 A from-scratch Verlet-integration constraint solver — no Box2D, no Matter.js, no physics library at all.
  2. 02 Local two-player duels: keyboard (split control scheme) or on-screen touch controls throw punches, kicks, and lunges.
  3. 03 Procedurally varied arenas — tilted floors and floating platforms, driven by a seed so a rematch never plays out the same way twice.
  4. 04 A full juice pass: screen shake, an impact flash at the point of contact, hitstop, synthesized WebAudio SFX, and a win-celebration overlay with a K.O. stamp.
